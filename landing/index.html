How it works

Three simple steps — no accounts, no setup, no cloud.

The tiny CC icon pops up near your cursor after copying text.
1

Copy anything

Select text in any app and press Ctrl+C. A tiny CC icon pops up near your cursor.

Left-click the CC icon to save the text to your collection.
2

Click to save

Left-click the CC icon to confirm. Ignore it for 3 seconds and it disappears — nothing is saved.

All saved items appear in the collection manager, ready to reorder and paste.
3

Paste all in order

Open the collection manager (Ctrl+Shift+C), arrange items, and paste everything at once.

Features

Designed for researchers, writers, and anyone who collects text.

Works in any app

Word, Chrome, PDF readers, WPS, VS Code, terminals — if you can copy text, Copy Collector can save it.

100% local & private

No servers, no accounts, no API calls. All data stays on your machine in a local JSON file.

Drag to reorder

Drag items up or down in the collection manager. Auto-scroll when you reach the edge.

Search & filter

Press Ctrl+F to search your collection. Matching text is highlighted as you type.

Session history

Complete a session to archive your collection. Browse past sessions with their own copy options.

Two themes

Dark glossy black (matching the CC icon) or clean white. Press Ctrl+D to toggle anytime.
